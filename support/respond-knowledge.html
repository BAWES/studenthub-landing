StudentHub is a recruitment platform that connects businesses with student workers, providing part-time job opportunities for students and fresh graduates in Kuwait. üëî

What is StudentHub?

StudentHub is your premium all-inclusive, comprehensive job-placement portal designed to kickstart your professional career. We offer a platform consisting of multiple apps to facilitate effective recruitment, ensuring the right person is working at the right place at the right time. Our collaboration with the Ministry of Youth and the Ministry of Manpower makes StudentHub a legal way to hire part-timers in Kuwait. We handle all the legalities, ensuring compliance with labor laws.

The StudentHub Story

StudentHub was established to become the legal way to hire part-timers in Kuwait. Our journey began with the simple idea of connecting employers with students for part-time job opportunities. Through partnerships with the Ministry of Youth and the Ministry of Manpower, we have made it legal for businesses to hire part-time employees. We also collaborate with the Ministry of Health to issue health cards, allowing our staff to work in the F&B industry.

What We Offer

We provide on-demand recruitment of students and complete recruitment solutions including sourcing, time-tracking, scheduling, payment processing, and payroll. Our team acts as your HR team, managing everything from recruitment to payroll.

To reset your password on StudentHub, follow these steps:

Steps to Reset Your Password

  1. Go to the StudentHub website and click on "Forgot your password?"
  1. Enter the email address you registered with and click on "Send Reset Instructions".
  1. Check your email for a reset link and open it.
  1. Enter a new password and repeat it to confirm.
  1. Click on "Update Password" to finalize the reset process.

Please note that the collection time is between 12:00 pm and 2:00 pm.
